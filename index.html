    Why Precast?

  • Easy and Quick Installation.
  • Better Quality Control.
  • Minimal disruption on site.
  • Independent of construction schedule/bottlenecks.

    • Cast-in-Situ vs Precast

    Proposed Precast Products

    All of them can be used with conventional/monolithic construction

  • Precast Partition Walls.
  • Precast Staircase
  • Finished Bathroom Pod
  • Precast Boundary Walls.
  • Precast Column,Beams,Slabs.

    • Precast Partition Walls

  • All internal partition walls can be done with precast.
  • Electrical Conduits and boxes can be fitted during casting.
  • Door frames can also be fitted during casting.
  • No need to do masonry and plastering.
  • Can reduce the finishing time to great extent.

    • Precast Column,Beams,Slabs

  • Podium works delay the project.
  • Podium slab can also be pre-casted.
  • Longer spans are also possible up to 30m.
  • Same can be expedited by using precast columns and beams.

    • Finished Bathroom Pod

  • Bathroom involve- Waterproofing,Plumbing,Tiling,Electrical,Joinery works(door & ventilation).
  • Considerable time can be saved by using precast Bathroom Pods.
  • Almost all trades are involved in a small space of 40 sqft.
  • installation time @site-30-4 min/pod.
  • Plug and play solution.

    • Precast Staircase

  • Considerable time can be saved by using precast staircase.
  • Installation time < 30 min.

    • Precast Boundary Walls

  • Easy to install.
  • No tilting/cracks issues.
  • Can retain earth load as well.
  • Any kind of serface finish is possible.
